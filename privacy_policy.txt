=================================================================
Privacy Policy
=================================================================

Tabs Outliner collect anonymous usage statistics, in agregated way and 
without any personally identifiable information. So it technically will be
impossible to identify you or in any way associate the usage statistic
with your persona.

The information that is collected contain frequency of usage
of different Tabs Outliner features, and technical details like memory,
CPU or disk utilizations during their usage. It's will be used to improve
the product, its performance, better memory management and to discover
and in advance resolve possible problems that might arise during Chrome
updates.

Collected information in no way contain the visited links,
their titles or your notes.
